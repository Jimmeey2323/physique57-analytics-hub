From the Ashes: Actionable Edicts

This chaos cannot stand. We must forge order from this data-driven dumpster fire. Here are the decrees.

Decree 1: Operation: Restore the Goddess

Problem: Our star player, Mrigakshi, has lost her mojo in Bandra.
Solution: We must figure out why. Is it the client demographic? The studio vibe? She will immediately shadow Anisha and Simran's classes in Bandra to decode the local dialect of sales. Failure is not an option. We need our sorceress back.

Decree 2: The Reshma Conundrum

Problem: Reshma is running a wildly popular soup kitchen for fitness.
Solution: Her charisma is a gold mine we are failing to excavate. For the next month, a top converter (like Simonelle) will co-teach her classes. Reshma will handle the workout, and the co-trainer will handle the close. We will monetize her popularity, by force if necessary.

Decree 3: The Ghostbusters Protocol

Problem: We have trainers who are either barely working or statistically non-existent.
Solution: An immediate audit of the schedules for Sovena, Vivaran, Raunak, Bret, and others with minimal sessions. We need to consolidate class slots and give them to proven performers like Simran. We are running a Supreme HQ, not a casual drop-in center for trainers.

This report has been sealed and classified under Project Obsidian. The truth is harsh, but the numbers are unforgiving. Let's transmute these failures into fuel for December.
