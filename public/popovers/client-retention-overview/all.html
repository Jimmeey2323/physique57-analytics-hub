📈 Monthly Trend

January delivered an exceptional 65.5% conversion rate — by far the highest single-month conversion of any location across the entire network in Q1. This reflects the quality of the audience at Pop-up events and the immediacy of the paid-entry model. February and March show a natural moderation as the novelty effect settles, but both months still delivered conversion rates (34.6% and 31.8%) that significantly outperform any permanent studio. The Pop-up channel is proving to be the most commercially efficient new client acquisition model in the portfolio.

Highest Conversion Rate in the Entire Network
At 45.5%, the Pop-up channel converts nearly 1 in 2 new clients — the highest conversion rate of any location. The paid-entry and on-site purchase model filters for high-intent clients and removes the typical trial-to-commitment barrier.
💰
January's 65.5% — a Network-Wide Benchmark
January's conversion rate at Pop-up (65.5%) is the highest single-month rate recorded anywhere in the portfolio in Q1 2026. This performance defines what is possible when the right audience, experience, and offer come together simultaneously.
⚡
Immediate Conversion — Zero Lag
Average conversion speed of effectively 0 days (same-day purchasing) at Pop-up confirms that when clients are engaged on-site, the barriers to purchase are minimal. The in-person, high-energy pop-up format is uniquely suited to driving instant decision-making.
🔗
Referral Outlier — Extraordinary LTV Signal
A single referral client at Pop-up generated avg LTV of ₹42,784 and 63 visits — an extreme outlier that nonetheless demonstrates the power of high-affinity referrals in this channel. Even 2–3 such referral clients per quarter would be transformative.
📲
Dashboard & Abandoned Checkout Suggest Digital Recovery Opportunity
27 leads from 'Dashboard' and 10 from 'Abandoned Checkout' are already in the digital funnel — they just need the right push. Automated recovery sequences for these warm leads (reminder WhatsApp, re-engagement offer) could capture 5–8 additional conversions per month.
🎪
Hosted Class Conversion Needs Work Even Here
9 hosted class visitors at Pop-up yielded 0 conversions — even in this high-conversion environment. This reinforces that hosted class visitors need a dedicated post-event follow-up regardless of the venue or format.

Q2 2026 Recommendations
Strategic Action Plan
Prioritised actions to maximise new client conversion and retention heading into Q2.
  • 1
    Scale Pop-up Event Frequency to Multiply Revenue: With a 45.5% conversion rate and same-day LTV, each Pop-up event is one of the most commercially efficient acquisition events in the business. Increasing from current frequency to one additional event per month would yield approximately 25+ new conversions per month at current rates.
  • 2
    Replicate January's Model Throughout Q2: January's 65.5% conversion at Pop-up was driven by specific conditions — a particular event format, audience, and location. Analyse and document what made January exceptional (event partner? time of day? team present?) and attempt to replicate those conditions deliberately in Q2 events.
  • 3
    Assign Shruti Kulkarni as Pop-up Conversion Lead: Shruti converts 77.8% of new clients she sees at Pop-up events — an extraordinary rate. Ensuring her presence at all major Pop-up activations in Q2, and having her mentor other trainers on her in-event conversion approach, will lift the overall Pop-up conversion rate.
  • 4
    Build a Referral Activation at Pop-up Events: The single referral client at Pop-up generated ₹42,784 LTV. Actively generating referrals at Pop-up events — through an on-site 'Bring a Friend' incentive, shared social content, or QR-based referral links — could yield 3–5 high-LTV referral clients per quarter.
  • 5
    Deploy Automated Recovery for Abandoned Checkout & Dashboard Leads: 10 abandoned checkout leads and 27 dashboard leads represent clients who were close to purchasing but did not complete. A same-day automated WhatsApp recovery sequence ('You were so close — here's your spot!') with a clear CTA could recover 3–5 additional conversions from these warm leads per event.
