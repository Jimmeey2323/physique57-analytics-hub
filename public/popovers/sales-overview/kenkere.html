📊 Monthly Trend Observation

Kenkere House demonstrates a remarkable and encouraging trend: member counts grew every single month, from 95 in January to 113 in February and 123 in March — a 29% growth in active members across just three months. Transaction volumes also rose (186 → 208 → 224), confirming growing client engagement. The revenue softening across months reflects a natural product mix shift as new members come in at introductory price points. As these members mature and migrate toward full memberships and packages, the revenue trajectory is expected to strengthen materially in Q2 and beyond.

Strategic Insights
Key Findings & Opportunities
Data-driven analysis to inform Kenkere House's Q2 strategic priorities.
📈
Fastest Growing Member Base
From 95 active members in January to 123 in March — a 29% increase in just one quarter — Kenkere House is demonstrating the strongest member growth trajectory in the portfolio. This foundation will power compounding revenue in Q2 and Q3.
💚
Best-in-Class Discount Discipline
With just 4.4% average discount and only 9.1% of transactions discounted, Kenkere House has the healthiest pricing discipline in the network. This preserves margin and signals strong perceived value for the product offering.
🔄
Renewal Rate Has Upside Potential
Renewals account for 43.7% of transactions vs. the network leader at 63.2%. Given the strong influx of new members, proactive renewal outreach 2 weeks before expiry — through personalised communication — can meaningfully lift renewal conversion in Q2.
🛍️
Retail Volume is the Second Highest
226 retail transactions is a strong indicator of engaged members who see Kenkere House as part of their lifestyle. Structured retail programmes and seasonal launches can convert this volume into a more meaningful revenue stream.
🎯
Annual Membership Penetration Opportunity
Annual Unlimited generated ₹1.56L — below its potential given the growing member base. Converting even 10% of monthly members to annual plans would generate a significant LTV uplift and reduce monthly churn exposure.
👥
Strong Two-Person Sales Engine
Api Serou and Prathap KP together drove ₹16.24L — over 64% of total location revenue. This tight, high-performing duo is a major asset; ensuring their continuity and mentoring support staff behind them is a key operational priority.
Strategic Recommendations
Q2 2026 Action Plan
Prioritised initiatives to accelerate Kenkere House's momentum and maximise Q2 outcomes.
  • 1
    Activate a Proactive Renewal Programme: With renewals at 43.7%, there is a clear opportunity to deepen loyalty. Implement an automated renewal reminder system starting 14 days before membership expiry, combined with a "Loyal Member Reward" — a value-add such as a complimentary retail item or class credit for re-committing early. This can push renewals toward the 55–60% range in Q2.
  • 2
    Convert Growing March Member Base into Revenue: With 123 active members in March — the highest point of the quarter — Q2 starts from a strong base. Launching March with package upgrade campaigns (e.g., "Step Up from Monthly to 3-Month") will monetise this growing cohort at higher ticket values.
  • 3
    Drive Annual Membership Conversion: Only ₹1.56L came from Annual Unlimited — significantly below its potential. A dedicated "Go Annual" campaign offering a compelling incentive (free months, complimentary private class, or retail credit) targeted at 3-month and monthly members could generate ₹5–8L of high-LTV revenue in Q2 alone.
  • 4
    Capitalise on the Retail Volume with Curated Offers: 226 retail transactions is excellent engagement. Introduce a member loyalty card for retail spend, or bundle retail items into membership renewal incentives. This converts incidental purchases into habitual ones, driving meaningful retail revenue growth.
  • 5
    Grow Private Classes from the Strong Membership Base: With ₹1.16L from just 16 private transactions, the average private class value is compelling. Targeted outreach to premium 3-month and annual members about private training can grow this segment significantly in Q2.
  • 6
    Sustain & Reward the Core Sales Team: Api Serou and Prathap KP are delivering exceptional results. Formalising a recognition and incentive structure will retain their motivation, while also providing a development pathway for support staff to grow into similar roles — building team depth and reducing key-person dependency.
