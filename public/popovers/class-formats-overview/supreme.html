The Two Titans & The Missing Pillar

A Strategic Analysis of Class Format Performance at Supreme HQ

Based on All-Time & November 2025 Data

Executive Summary: From Powerhouse to Unbeatable

The performance data for Supreme HQ is unequivocal: this is not a studio in need of fixing, but a powerhouse poised for market domination. Your business is built on two titans of fitness, `Barre` and `PowerCycle`, both of which operate at an elite >90% fill rate and have demonstrated spectacular year-over-year revenue growth. This is a story of overwhelming success.

However, the data also reveals the single greatest opportunity for exponential growth: the complete absence of a dedicated `Strength` training program. While you are dominating the cardio and sculpt markets, you are leaving millions in revenue on the table by not serving the massive, and complementary, demand for strength training. The strategic mandate is to optimize the operations of your two existing titans while launching the missing third pillar to create an unassailable fitness trifecta.

The 15-Point Blueprint for Market Dominance

I. Optimize the Titans (The A → A+ Plan)

  1. 1.
    Establish "Headliner" Status for A-List Instructors.

    Rationale: The data shows a clear performance gap between instructors. Elevate top performers like Vivaran Dhasmana (Barre/PowerCycle), Rohan Dahima (Barre), and Cauveri Vikrant (PowerCycle) to "Headliner" status. Give them the most desirable prime-time slots, exclusively.

  2. 2.
    Prune Underperforming Prime-Time Slots.

    Rationale: Even with a 90%+ average, some trainers are lagging. A trainer with a 75% fill rate in a prime evening slot is leaving money on the table. Move these instructors to off-peak times or mentorship roles and replace them with a proven Headliner.

  3. 3.
    Introduce "Barre Advanced" and "PowerCycle Pro" Classes.

    Rationale: Your clients are loyal and getting stronger. To prevent plateaus and churn, create a higher-intensity "Level 2" class for each format once a week. This creates an aspirational goal and retains your most advanced members.

  4. 4.
    Increase Session Capacity or Price for Top Slots.

    Rationale: A consistent 100% fill rate means demand is outstripping supply. If fire codes permit, add one more spot. If not, consider implementing a small "Prime Time" premium of ₹100-200 for these specific, always-waitlisted classes.

  5. 5.
    Analyze the Revenue-per-Session Metric.

    Rationale: A trainer might have a high fill rate but a low revenue-per-session if their class is filled with legacy/discounted members. Use this metric to identify which instructors attract your most profitable clients and learn from their approach.

II. Launch The Third Pillar (The `Strength` Initiative)

  1. 6.
    Greenlight a `Strength` Program Pilot Immediately.

    Rationale: This is the single largest growth opportunity available. The market demand for effective strength training is enormous. You are leaving a massive revenue stream completely untapped.

  2. 7.
    Acquire or Develop `Strength`-Specific Talent.

    Rationale: Do not simply assign a `Barre` instructor to teach strength. Hire a certified strength coach or identify a current trainer with a legitimate strength background (like Atulan Purohit at Kwality House) to lead the program. Authenticity is non-negotiable.

  3. 8.
    Launch with a "Foundations of Strength" Workshop Series.

    Rationale: Introduce the new format as a premium, educational experience. A paid 4-week workshop on core lifts (squat, deadlift, press) will generate upfront revenue and build a confident, competent client base before launching drop-in classes.

  4. 9.
    Create a "Phase 1" Schedule.

    Rationale: Don't flood the schedule. Start with 4-6 carefully selected time slots on weekday mornings and evenings, plus one on Saturday. Measure performance obsessively for 90 days before expanding.

  5. 10.
    Position `Strength` as the Essential Complement.

    Rationale: Market the new program directly to your existing clients. "Love `Barre`? Build the muscle that makes every plié stronger." "Love `PowerCycle`? Develop the power that drives every climb."

III. Create an Unbeatable Ecosystem (The Synergy)

  1. 11.
    Launch the "Supreme Trifecta" Package.

    Rationale: Once `Strength` is established, create a premium membership tier or class pack that specifically encourages members to engage with all three pillars. This builds well-rounded athletes and deepens their dependency on your studio.

  2. 12.
    Develop Curated Weekly "Training Plans".

    Rationale: Guide your members. Post sample weekly schedules in the studio and online: "Goal: Build Muscle? Try 2x Strength, 1x PowerCycle, 1x Barre." This adds value and intelligently cross-sells your formats.

  3. 13.
    Host "Fusion" Pop-Up Classes.

    Rationale: Once a month, host a special 75-minute class like "Ride & Reps" (30 min cycle, 30 min strength) or "Barre & Bells" (Barre with kettlebells). This creates excitement and demonstrates how the formats complement each other.

  4. 14.
    Create an "All-Access" Pricing Tier.

    Rationale: If `Strength` classes are smaller and more coaching-intensive, they may warrant a higher price. Your top-tier unlimited membership should be the "All-Access Pass" that includes everything, making it the most aspirational and valuable option.

  5. 15.
    Re-Evaluate the Portfolio Annually.

    Rationale: Your success with `Barre` and `PowerCycle` is the result of excellent product-market fit. With `Strength` added, you will have a new baseline. Commit to an annual review to ensure all three pillars are performing at an elite level.
