Friday underperforms at 25% fill with a 27.8% late cancel rate — the studio's weakest scheduling day. The singular 90-day focus must be converting Rohan Dahima's 5-session demand into a structured expanded offering while simultaneously enforcing late-cancel policy on Monday/Thursday evening slots.

  5. Kajol Kanchan Dominates Multi-Format Revenue. With 425 attendees across 60 sessions and ₹2.5L total, Kajol's format generates 3.6× Chaitanya Nahar's revenue — strong case for prioritising her timeslot allocation in scheduling decisions.
